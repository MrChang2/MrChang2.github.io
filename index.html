What is Text Racer?

Why play Text Racer?

In an evergrowing mobile industry, the use of phones has become extremely commonplace. In such a world, texting is equally, if not more, important thant typing on a keyboard. As such, mastering the skill of texting is a nearly essential, something that this app will help you do!
